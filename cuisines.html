Cuisines of El Salvador

Discover the delicious flavors of the Land of Volcanoes

Pupusas

The most famous Salvadoran dish, pupusas are thick corn tortillas stuffed with cheese, beans, or meat.

Yuca Frita

Crispy fried cassava served with a tangy cabbage slaw and salsa, a popular side dish or snack.

Casamiento

A traditional combination of rice and beans, often served with eggs, cheese, or tortillas.
